Before you can interact with the AgrospAI Portal, you need to set up a Web3 wallet and connect it to the platform. This guide will walk you through the complete setup process.

## What You'll Need

- A web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Brave)
- MetaMask or another Web3 wallet extension
- A few minutes to complete the setup

## Setting Up MetaMask

MetaMask is the most popular Web3 wallet and is fully supported by the AgrospAI Portal.

<Steps>
<Step title="Download MetaMask">
  Visit the [official MetaMask website](https://metamask.io) and download the browser extension for your preferred browser.

  <Warning>
    Only download MetaMask from the official website to avoid phishing attempts and malicious extensions.
  </Warning>

  Once installed, you'll see the MetaMask fox icon in your browser's extension toolbar.
</Step>

<Step title="Create a new wallet">
  Click on the MetaMask extension icon and follow the setup wizard:

  1. Click **Get Started**
  2. Choose **Create a Wallet**
  3. Agree to the terms and conditions
  4. Create a strong password for your wallet

  <Note>
    This password encrypts your wallet data locally. You'll need it every time you unlock MetaMask.
  </Note>
</Step>

<Step title="Secure your Secret Recovery Phrase">
  MetaMask will present you with a 12-word Secret Recovery Phrase:

  1. **Write it down** on paper and store it in a safe place
  2. **Never share** your recovery phrase with anyone
  3. **Never store it** digitally (no screenshots, no cloud storage)
  4. Confirm your recovery phrase by selecting the words in the correct order

  <Warning>
    Your Secret Recovery Phrase is the master key to your wallet. Anyone with access to it can control your assets. If you lose it, you cannot recover your wallet.
  </Warning>
</Step>

<Step title="Complete the setup">
  After confirming your recovery phrase, your MetaMask wallet is ready! You'll see your account with a default name like "Account 1" and your wallet address.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting to AgrospAI Portal

Once your wallet is set up, you can connect it to the AgrospAI Portal.

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to the portal">
  Open the AgrospAI Portal in your browser. If this is your first time visiting, you'll see an onboarding guide.
</Step>

<Step title="Click Connect Wallet">
  Look for the **Connect Wallet** button in the top right corner of the portal and click it.

  A MetaMask popup will appear asking for permission to connect.
</Step>

<Step title="Authorize the connection">
  Review the permissions being requested:

  - Read your public wallet address
  - View your account balance
  - See your activity on the network
  - Suggest transactions for your approval

  Click **Connect** to authorize the portal.

  <Note>
    The portal cannot perform transactions without your explicit approval. Each transaction will require you to confirm it in MetaMask.
  </Note>
</Step>

<Step title="Verify the connection">
  Once connected, you'll see:
  - Your truncated wallet address (e.g., `0x1234...5678`) in the top right corner
  - Your account balance
  - Access to all portal features
</Step>
</Steps>

## Configuring the Network

The AgrospAI Portal operates on specific blockchain networks. You may need to add or switch networks.

<Steps>
<Step title="Add the network">
  When you first connect, the portal may prompt you to add the required network to MetaMask.

  Click **Approve** when MetaMask asks to add the network.
</Step>

<Step title="Switch networks (if needed)">
  If you're on the wrong network:

  1. Click the network selector at the top of MetaMask
  2. Select the correct network for the AgrospAI Portal
  3. The portal will automatically detect the network change
</Step>

<Step title="Add custom tokens">
  To interact with the portal, you'll need to import custom tokens:

  1. The portal will display a button to **Import Custom Tokens**
  2. Click the button to add the EURAU payment token to MetaMask
  3. Confirm the token import in MetaMask

  <Note>
    Pontus-X uses EURAU as both a network token (for gas fees) and as a payment token for data transactions.
  </Note>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Getting Test Tokens

For testing purposes, you can obtain test tokens from the faucet.

<Steps>
<Step title="Access the faucet">
  In the portal's onboarding guide or settings, look for the **Claim Test Tokens** option.
</Step>

<Step title="Request tokens">
  Click the button to request test tokens. The portal will:
  1. Connect to the faucet service
  2. Send test EURAU tokens to your wallet
  3. Display a confirmation once complete
</Step>

<Step title="Verify token receipt">
  Check your MetaMask wallet to confirm you've received the test tokens. You should see the EURAU balance updated.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Alternative Wallets

While MetaMask is recommended, the AgrospAI Portal supports any Web3 wallet that implements the standard Ethereum provider interface, including:

- **WalletConnect** - Connect mobile wallets by scanning a QR code
- **Coinbase Wallet** - Integrated with Coinbase exchange
- **Brave Wallet** - Built into the Brave browser
- **Trust Wallet** - Popular mobile wallet

The connection process is similar to MetaMask for all supported wallets.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="MetaMask won't connect">
  - Make sure you've unlocked MetaMask by entering your password
  - Try refreshing the portal page
  - Check if MetaMask is on the correct network
  - Clear your browser cache and try again
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Wrong network error">
  The portal will prompt you to switch networks. Click **Switch Network** in the MetaMask popup, or manually select the correct network from the MetaMask extension.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Transactions failing">
  - Ensure you have enough network tokens (EURAU) for gas fees
  - Check that you're on the correct network
  - Try increasing the gas limit in MetaMask
  - Wait a few moments and try again if the network is congested
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can't see my tokens">
  You may need to manually import token contracts:
  1. Copy the token contract address from the portal
  2. Open MetaMask and click **Import Tokens**
  3. Paste the contract address
  4. Click **Add Custom Token**
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security Best Practices

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Protect Your Seed Phrase" icon="lock">
  Never share your 12-word recovery phrase. Store it offline in a secure location.
</Card>

<Card title="Verify Transactions" icon="shield-check">
  Always review transaction details in MetaMask before confirming. Check the recipient address and amount.
</Card>

<Card title="Use Hardware Wallets" icon="usb-drive">
  For large amounts or long-term storage, consider using a hardware wallet like Ledger or Trezor.
</Card>

<Card title="Beware of Phishing" icon="triangle-exclamation">
  Only connect your wallet to trusted sites. Check the URL carefully before connecting.
</Card>
</CardGroup>
