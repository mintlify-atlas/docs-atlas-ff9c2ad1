The AgrospAI Portal provides a marketplace where you can discover, purchase, and access data assets published by other users.

## Discovering Data Assets

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to the search page">
  From the portal homepage, you'll see featured datasets and a search interface.

  The main search area allows you to explore all available data assets.
</Step>

<Step title="Search for assets">
  Use the search functionality to find relevant data:

  ### Search Methods

  - **Keyword Search**: Enter terms in the search bar
    - Searches across titles, descriptions, tags, and authors
    - Example searches: "agriculture", "climate data", "yield prediction"

  - **Browse by Category**: Filter by data categories
    - Dataset types (time-series, geospatial, etc.)
    - Industry domains (agriculture, energy, healthcare)

  - **Filter Results**: Refine your search using filters:
    - Asset type (Dataset or Algorithm)
    - Pricing (Free or Paid)
    - Network/Chain
    - Access type (Download or Compute)
</Step>

<Step title="Sort results">
  Organize search results by:
  - **Newest**: Recently published assets
  - **Relevance**: Best match to your search terms
  - **Most Popular**: Based on download/usage count
  - **Price**: Low to high or high to low
</Step>

<Step title="Preview asset details">
  Click on any asset to view its details page:

  ### Asset Information

  - **Title and Description**: What the data contains
  - **Author/Publisher**: Who published the data
  - **Price**: Cost per access (or "Free")
  - **Type**: Dataset or Algorithm
  - **Tags**: Keywords and categories
  - **Statistics**: Number of downloads, compute jobs run
  - **Network**: Blockchain network where it's published

  ### Technical Details

  - **DID**: Decentralized Identifier (unique asset ID)
  - **Datatoken**: Access token contract address
  - **NFT Owner**: Current owner of the data asset
  - **License**: Usage terms and license information
  - **Timeout**: Access validity period (if applicable)

  ### Sample Data Preview

  Some assets may provide:
  - File preview or sample rows
  - Data schema information
  - Format specifications
  - Example outputs
</Step>
</Steps>

## Purchasing Access

Once you've found a data asset you want to access, you need to purchase access rights.

<Steps>
<Step title="Connect your wallet">
  Make sure your [wallet is connected](/guides/wallet-setup) and you're on the correct network.

  The asset page will display your current network and prompt you to switch if needed.
</Step>

<Step title="Review pricing and terms">
  On the asset detail page, review:

  ### Price Breakdown

  The portal displays the full cost including:
  - Base asset price
  - Ocean Protocol fees
  - Marketplace fees
  - Gas fees (estimated)
  - **Total cost** in the payment token (usually EURAU)

  ### Terms and Conditions

  You must accept:
  - **Portal Terms**: General platform terms
  - **Asset License**: Specific terms for this data asset

  Read both carefully before proceeding.

  <Warning>
    Once you purchase access, the transaction cannot be reversed. Make sure you understand the terms and pricing.
  </Warning>
</Step>

<Step title="Check your balance">
  Ensure your wallet has sufficient:
  - Payment tokens (EURAU) for the asset price
  - Network tokens (EURAU) for gas fees

  If your balance is insufficient:
  - The purchase button will be disabled
  - A message will indicate what you need
  - You can acquire tokens through exchanges or faucets (for testnets)
</Step>

<Step title="Review consumer parameters (if applicable)">
  Some assets allow you to customize access parameters:

  - Date ranges for time-series data
  - Geographic regions for geospatial data
  - Data formats or processing options
  - Algorithm-specific settings

  Configure these according to your needs.
</Step>

<Step title="Initiate purchase">
  Click the **Buy** or **Purchase** button.

  ### What Happens:

  1. **Price Calculation**: Portal calculates final price with fees
  2. **MetaMask Opens**: Transaction prompt appears
  3. **Review Transaction**: Check the amount and recipient
  4. **Confirm**: Approve the transaction in MetaMask
  5. **Wait for Confirmation**: Transaction is processed on-chain

  <Note>
    For priced assets, this requires a payment transaction. For free assets, it creates an access record.
  </Note>
</Step>

<Step title="Wait for confirmation">
  The portal displays transaction progress:
  - "Ordering asset..."
  - "Waiting for confirmation..."
  - "Success!"

  This typically takes 10-30 seconds, depending on network conditions.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Downloading Data

After purchasing access, you can download the data.

<Steps>
<Step title="Access your purchased asset">
  After a successful purchase, the asset page updates to show:
  - **Download** button (for datasets)
  - **Start Compute** button (for compute-enabled assets)
  - Your access transaction details
  - Remaining validity period (if timeout applies)
</Step>

<Step title="Download the data">
  Click the **Download** button:

  1. Portal verifies your access rights
  2. Requests decryption from the provider
  3. Initiates file download
  4. Your browser saves the file

  ### Download Process

  - The download is authenticated using your wallet signature
  - Data is decrypted on-the-fly by the provider service
  - You receive the original, unencrypted file
  - Download progress shows in your browser

  <Note>
    For large files, the download may take several minutes. Don't close the browser window.
  </Note>
</Step>

<Step title="Verify the downloaded data">
  After download completes:
  - Check the file size and format
  - Verify data integrity
  - Review contents match the description
  - Test with your intended use case
</Step>
</Steps>

## Accessing Purchased Assets Later

You don't need to download immediately after purchase.

### From Your Profile

1. Navigate to your **Profile** page
2. Go to the **Downloads** section
3. Find your purchased asset
4. Click **Download** to access it again

### Access Validity

- **No Timeout**: Download as many times as you want
- **With Timeout**: Download within the validity period
  - The timeout duration is shown on the asset page
  - After expiration, you need to purchase access again

### Redownloading

- Most purchases allow multiple downloads
- Each download uses the same access token (no additional cost)
- As long as your access is valid, you can redownload

## Using Algorithms

Algorithms work differently from datasets.

<Steps>
<Step title="Purchase algorithm access">
  The purchase process is the same as for datasets.
</Step>

<Step title="View compatible datasets">
  On the algorithm asset page, you'll see:
  - **Compatible Datasets**: Datasets this algorithm can run on
  - Each dataset shows if you have access
  - Purchase dataset access if needed
</Step>

<Step title="Run the algorithm">
  After purchasing both algorithm and dataset access:

  1. Navigate to the dataset's compute page
  2. Select your purchased algorithm
  3. Configure any algorithm parameters
  4. Click **Start Compute Job**
  5. Wait for results (see [Compute-to-Data guide](/guides/compute-to-data))
</Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Access Types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Direct Download">
    ### Download Access

    The default access method for datasets:

    <Check>
      **Advantages**
      - Full access to the raw data
      - Use data offline with your own tools
      - No additional compute costs
      - Works with any processing software
    </Check>

    <Warning>
      **Considerations**
      - You download the complete dataset
      - Data leaves the publisher's control
      - You're responsible for data security
      - May require storage space for large files
    </Warning>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Compute-to-Data">
    ### Compute Access

    Privacy-preserving access method:

    <Check>
      **Advantages**
      - Data never leaves the provider's environment
      - Publisher maintains data privacy
      - Access controlled and auditable
      - Results-only model (you get outputs, not raw data)
    </Check>

    <Warning>
      **Considerations**
      - Cannot access raw data directly
      - Must use approved algorithms
      - Additional compute costs apply
      - Processing time depends on job queue
    </Warning>

    Learn more in the [Compute-to-Data guide](/guides/compute-to-data).
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="SaaS Access">
    ### Software as a Service

    Some assets provide API or service access:

    <Check>
      **Advantages**
      - Continuous access to data service
      - No download needed
      - Real-time or streaming data
      - Integrated into your applications
    </Check>

    <Info>
      After purchasing, click **Go to Service** to access the external service URL. You'll be redirected to the provider's platform.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Access Control and Whitelisting

Some assets have access restrictions.

### Publisher Whitelist

If an asset uses a whitelist:
- Only approved wallet addresses can purchase access
- The portal shows: "You are not whitelisted for this asset"
- Contact the publisher to request access
- Once whitelisted, you can purchase normally

### Blacklist

If you're on a blacklist:
- You cannot purchase or access the asset
- The portal prevents transactions
- This is at the publisher's discretion

### Your Whitelisting Status

The asset page displays:
- **Whitelisted**: You have explicit access
- **Public Access**: No restrictions
- **Not Whitelisted**: You cannot access this asset

## Payment Tokens

Different assets may use different payment tokens:

### Default Token: EURAU

Most assets on the AgrospAI Portal use EURAU:
- The primary payment token for the platform
- Also used for network gas fees
- Obtain through exchanges or portal faucet (testnet)

### Other Tokens

Some publishers may accept:
- OCEAN tokens
- Stablecoins (USDC, DAI)
- Other ERC-20 tokens

The asset page displays which tokens are accepted.

### Token Approval

For ERC-20 payments:
1. First transaction: Approve the marketplace to spend your tokens
2. Second transaction: Complete the purchase

Both require MetaMask confirmation.

## Viewing Your Downloads

Track all your purchased assets from your profile.

### Downloads History

1. Go to your **Profile** page
2. Navigate to **History** → **Downloads**
3. See all assets you've purchased:
   - Asset name and type
   - Purchase date and transaction
   - Current access status
   - Download button (if still valid)
   - Time remaining (if timeout applies)

### Download Again

To redownload a previously purchased asset:
- Click the asset name to go to its page
- Or use the download button directly from your history

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Cannot purchase - insufficient balance">
  - Check your EURAU balance in MetaMask
  - You need tokens for both the price and gas fees
  - Acquire more tokens through exchanges or the faucet
  - Try adjusting gas price settings in MetaMask
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Purchase succeeded but cannot download">
  - Wait a moment for transaction confirmation
  - Refresh the page
  - Check you're still connected with the same wallet
  - Verify the asset page shows your access
  - Try from your Profile → Downloads instead
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Download fails or file corrupted">
  - Check your internet connection
  - Try downloading again (no additional cost)
  - Ensure you have enough disk space
  - Try a different browser if issues persist
  - Contact the asset publisher if file is consistently broken
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Access expired">
  - Some assets have timeout periods
  - After expiration, you must purchase again
  - Check the timeout duration before purchasing
  - Download immediately if you need long-term access
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Not whitelisted for restricted asset">
  - The publisher has restricted access
  - Look for publisher contact information
  - Request whitelist access
  - Or find alternative data assets
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
