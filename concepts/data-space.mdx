## What is a Data Space?

A data space is a decentralized ecosystem for secure and sovereign data sharing, built on the principles of data sovereignty, interoperability, and trust. AgrospAI implements the Gaia-X framework to create a specialized data space for the agri-food sector.

## Gaia-X Integration

AgrospAI Portal is built on the Gaia-X principles and integrates with the Gaia-X ecosystem through:

### Gaia-X Registry

The portal connects to official Gaia-X registries for participant verification:

```javascript
allowedGaiaXRegistryDomains: [
  'https://registry.gaia-x.eu/v2206',
  'https://registry.lab.gaia-x.eu/v2206'
]
```

### Service Credentials

Data assets published on AgrospAI can be exported as Gaia-X Service Credentials compatible with:

- **Gaia-X Digital Clearing Houses (GXDCH)** for credential verification
- **Gaia-X Trust Framework** for compliance validation
- **Gaia-X Service Credential specification 22.10**

## Data Sovereignty Principles

The portal implements key data sovereignty principles:

### 1. Owner Control

Data publishers maintain complete control over:
- Who can access their data
- How their data is priced
- Where their data is processed (Compute-to-Data)
- Terms and conditions of use

### 2. Verifiable Identity

All participants are identified through:
- **Gaia-X Participant Credentials** for organization identity
- **Legal Registration Numbers** with VAT validation
- **Verifiable Presentations** for cryptographic proof
